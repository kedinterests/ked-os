# KED Interests

## Overview

KED Interests is a web development and digital solutions company serving the oil & gas energy sector, with deep expertise in mineral rights, drilling operations, and industry-specific challenges.

## Market Focus

- Oil & gas energy sector
- Mineral rights management and transactions
- Drilling operations and industry workflows
- Energy sector professionals and companies

## Tech Philosophy

- Modern web technologies (Astro, Discourse, hand-coded sites)
- No WordPress — prioritizes performance and control
- Emphasis on specialization and domain expertise over generalist tools

## Team Structure

- **Kenny Dubose:** Founder, strategic direction
- **Chris Malone:** Lead developer, technical execution

## Key Differentiators

- Deep understanding of oil & gas workflows
- Custom web solutions tailored to industry needs
- Modern, performant technology stack
- Focus on solving real problems in the energy sector
